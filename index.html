Features

Everything a TAM needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built around the workflows TAMs and CSMs actually run — not a generic CRM with extra fields.

◎
Account Health Scoring
Real-time scores weighted across adoption, engagement, NPS, and support activity. Color-coded so portfolio status is readable at a glance.
⚡
Nova Signal Engine
AI-surfaced risk signals detect champion departures, adoption drops, and renewal gaps before they become problems — powered by SyncToScale data.
◷
Renewal Timeline View
See every renewal date across your portfolio in one view, with color-coded urgency so nothing sneaks up on you at 6 weeks out.
↑
Expansion Detection
Nova identifies accounts with high adoption and long runways — flagging upsell and expansion opportunities before the QBR conversation.
◈
Activity Feed
A unified log of touchpoints, support escalations, sync events, and account changes — so your full account history is always one scroll away.
◫
QBR Builder
Generate structured quarterly business review decks directly from account health data — pre-filled with adoption metrics, risk flags, and next steps.
How it works

From integration to insight
in three steps.

SyncDash pulls live workflow and adoption data from SyncToScale and surfaces what matters.

01
Connect via SyncToScale
SyncDash reads workflow execution data, trigger events, and integration activity directly from your SyncToScale account — no manual data entry.
02
Nova scores your accounts
The Nova signal engine analyzes adoption patterns, engagement gaps, and renewal timelines to generate a health score for every account in your portfolio.
03
You act on what matters
Prioritize your day from a single dashboard. Know which accounts need a check-in, which are expansion-ready, and which need an escalation plan now.
